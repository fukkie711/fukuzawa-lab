### Lab Equipment & Software

#### NVIDIA DGX Spark

![NVIDIA DGX Spark](/images/dgx2.jpg)

Our laboratory is equipped with the **NVIDIA DGX Spark**, a personal AI supercomputer powered by the NVIDIA GB10 Grace Blackwell Superchip. It delivers up to 1 PetaFLOPS of AI computing performance (FP4 precision) and features 128GB of unified system memory, enabling local fine-tuning and inference of large language models (LLMs) without relying on cloud services. For more details, see our [blog post](/posts/dgx-spark-2026/).

#### SPSS Statistics / AMOS Server

![SPSS Server](/images/server1.jpg)

We operate a rack-mounted **Windows Server** for statistical analysis. It runs **IBM SPSS Statistics** and **SPSS AMOS**, providing a shared computing environment for students conducting statistical research and thesis analysis.

#### Database Server

![Database Server](/images/server2.jpg)

We jointly operate a **Windows Server**-based database server with Professor Goto's laboratory, which specializes in intellectual property research. It is used for storing and managing large-scale datasets including patent databases, serving as a shared infrastructure across both laboratories.
